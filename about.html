About me

Hi! My name is Margaret Liu. I'm a San Francisco based designer with roots in NYC, Shanghai, Hawaii, and LA. I help people translate ideas into products and experiences. My design career started in architecture, but I have since moved from the physical to digital world.

Interesting facts about me:

I'm a professional pole dancer. I compete at least twice a year and travel the world teaching workshops! If there was any way I could have direct impact on the world, it's through teaching. My teaching style goes past technique and looks at the mental and emotional parts of dance.

I'm an anime fanatic. My favorites are Hunter X Hunter, Cowboy Bebop, and Samurai Champloo. The stories are complex, deep, and the character development is well designed in so many ways. The stickiest shows are the ones where you can grow with the characters and I take those learnings into designing products where users can grow with them.

In a past life, I was a mermaid. Sometimes I feel more comfortable in the water than on land. When I was diving regularly, I could hold my breath for minutes and free dive down about 60ft. I am inspired by the patterns, colors, and movement created underwater and use those in my visual and interaction designs.
